BREAKOUT SESSIONS

Deep dive into specific areas of youth mental health advocacy

Breaking Barriers and Promoting Youth Mental Health Equity

Moderator: Alyna Lim
Panelists: Nze Okoronta, Ross Layton, Dr. Sean Thum

Dismantling obstacles and forging pathways to ensure every youth has an equitable chance to thrive mentally and emotionally.

The Power of Connection: Fostering Social Support for Youth Wellbeing

Moderator: Glen Koh
Panelists: Marx Wang, Kate Barranco, Mercy Mkandawire

Exploring how to cultivate environments where meaningful relationships flourish and provide crucial safety nets.

Policy, Action, and Youth Mental Health: Why Grassroots Advocacy Matters for our Global Future

Moderator: Ronit Chakraborty
Panelists: Saanvi Arora, Connor Dalgaard, Emmy Egulu

Why grassroots advocacy matters for our global future and how local voices shape effective policies.
